About Me

Having earned my Ms in Marketing, I joined a small software firm where my role slowly evolved over the past two years. Ultimately, I was assigned the task of implementing staff software for a major client, and my success solidified my desire to formally transition into working as a software engineer.

My short-term career goal is to acquire the skills needed to lead a development team and create solutions that will help businesses reach their full potential. I want to become an experienced project manager who is capable of delivering multi-year projects by emphasizing effective communication with clients as well as other developers on the team.

My long-term career goal is to become an expert full-stack developer, mastering a wide range of technologies in order to adapt them quickly for new situations and make a meaningful impact in the domain. Ultimately, I want to build my own application and establish a startup using the management skills I acquired in business school. My professional aspiration is to use technology to improve how businesses function and how people interact, becoming a woman in tech who inspires women to further diversify the field.
